
> S4A allows for 6 analog inputs (A0,A1,A2,A3,A4,A5)
> 3 analog outputs (digital pins 5, 6 and 9)
> 2 digital inputs (digital pins 2 and 3)
> 3 digital outputs (pins 10, 11 and 13)
> 4 special outputs to connect Parallax continuous rotation servomotors i.e. digital pins 4, 7, 8 and 12

You can manage a board wirelessly by attaching an RF module to it, such as Xbee.
S4A allows you to control as many boards as USB ports you have.
